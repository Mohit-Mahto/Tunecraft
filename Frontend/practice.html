⚔ Mixing Jutsu Guide
🥁 Kick
Foundation of the beat. Keep it powerful at 80–100%.
🪘 Snare
Counter-attack on beats 2 and 4. Slightly quieter than kick.
🎩 Hi-Hat
The speed of your technique. Keep lighter at 50–70%.
🎸 Bass
Low-end power. Works with kick. Don't let them clash!
🎹 Synth
Your melody jutsu. Shine above rhythm without drowning it.
🎺 Brass
Adds warmth and punch. Great for fills and accents.
🎻 Strings
Emotional depth. Keep subtle unless it's a key moment.
🎤 Vocals
The story. Usually sits above everything — the star of the show.
