⚔ Mixer Jutsu Guide
🥁 Kick Drum
The foundation of your beat. Keep it loud (80–100%). It's your battle cry.
🪘 Snare
Hits on beats 2 and 4. Your counter-attack. Slightly quieter than the kick.
🎩 Hi-Hat
Creates the rhythm flow. Keep it lighter (50–70%). The speed of your technique.
🎸 Bass
Low-end power. Works with the kick. Don't let them clash — that's chakra overflow!
🎹 Synth
Your melody jutsu. Should shine above the rhythm without drowning it.
